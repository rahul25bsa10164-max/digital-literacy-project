During my research for this task, I was most surprised by how simple psychological tricks, like creating a sense of urgency, are more effective than high-tech hacking. Most UPI frauds happen because the victim is rushed into a decision. One habit I will personally change is my habit of clicking links in SMS messages. I will now manually navigate to the official website or app instead of trusting a link sent via text. This research has made me much more cautious about digital transactions.
