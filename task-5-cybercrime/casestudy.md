# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

## Part A: Case Study - UPI/Online Payment Fraud

**What the Crime Is:**
UPI Fraud involves deceiving users into sending money or revealing their PIN to scammers through fake payment links or QR codes.

**How it Typically Happens (Step-by-Step):**
1. **The Hook:** A scammer contacts the victim pretending to be a buyer or a customer care executive.
2. **The Deception:** They send a "Payment Request" or a QR code, claiming that the victim needs to scan it or enter their PIN to "receive" money.
3. **The Theft:** Once the victim enters their PIN, the money is instantly deducted from their bank account instead of being credited.
4. **The Disappearance:** The scammer immediately blocks the victim and moves the funds through multiple accounts to hide their trail.

**Target Audience:**
College students and young adults who frequently use apps like PhonePe, Google Pay, or Paytm for quick transactions are often targeted.

**Consequences:**
The primary consequence is the immediate loss of hard-earned money. Beyond financial loss, victims often experience a loss of trust in digital banking and significant mental stress.
