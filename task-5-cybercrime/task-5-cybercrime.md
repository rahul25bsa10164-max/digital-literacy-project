# 'Stay Safe Online' Prevention Checklist

1. **PIN Security:** Never enter your UPI PIN to "receive" money. A PIN is only required to send money.
2. **QR Code Caution:** Avoid scanning QR codes sent by strangers on WhatsApp or OLX.
3. **Official Apps:** Only use official banking apps and never share your OTP with anyone.
4. **Link Verification:** Do not click on suspicious links claiming you won a "lottery" or "cashback."
5. **Two-Factor Authentication:** Enable 2FA on all financial and social media accounts.
6. **Public Wi-Fi:** Avoid checking your bank balance or making payments while on public, unsecured Wi-Fi.
7. **Report Fraud:** If you are scammed, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal: **cybercrime.gov.in**.
8. **Emergency Helpline:** Call the National Helpline Number **1930** immediately to freeze the transaction.
